Our Story

Rooted in St. Pete. Committed to You.

Tyrone Chiropractic Clinic has been a cornerstone of the St. Petersburg community for over 50 years. Dr. Ric Lenholt, a St. Pete native, grew up here, went to school here, and chose to build his practice here — because this community means everything to him.

After graduating from Dixie High School and playing four years of varsity basketball at Eckerd College, Dr. Ric earned his Doctor of Chiropractic degree from Logan College in St. Louis before returning home to carry on the clinic's legacy. Today, he's not just your doctor — he's also coaching varsity basketball at St. Petersburg High School.

Family-centered practice — compassionate care for patients of all ages, from kids to seniors.

Expert in injury cases — established relationships with legal and insurance teams for smooth recovery.

Personalized care — Dr. Ric takes the time to understand your body and explain your treatment plan clearly.

Dr. Ric Lenholt, DC Owner · Logan College of Chiropractic

A St. Pete native and former college basketball player, Dr. Ric brings the same dedication to his patients that he brought to the court. He's known for making patients feel at ease while delivering exceptional, personalized chiropractic care.
